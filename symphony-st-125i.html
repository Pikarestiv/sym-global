Features

The Symphony ST adapts SYM smart cooling system with ABS in 125cc for more power, more stable, and safer maneuverability for urban drive, not to mention providing the better comfort as SYM has been pursuing for.

Having a seat height of 792 mm and 16 "wheels, the 125 ST offers the driver comfort and maneuverability during urban journeys. The LED positioning and LED rear lights give the Symphony ST a unique identity on the road.
