Features

Maxsym 400i, a combination of extraordinary engine power and artistic integral design, incarnates the consummate technology of SYM. The sharp dual projection headlights are of excellent aggression and safety. The excellent ergonomic setting offers outstanding comfort for long-distance cruising. SYM is proud to present this masterpiece, which offers dynamic, safe, and comfortable ride in and out the city.
