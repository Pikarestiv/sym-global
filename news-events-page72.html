SYM in Brussels Motor Show 2018

Home / News & Events / SYM in Brussels Motor Show 2018

The 12th two wheels fair has been held in Medellin Colombia this May, which is the second largest motor show in Latin America.63,000 visitors and 500 exhibitors who came from worldwide had joined this big party together. In this show, SYM had launched the new models: Jet 14 200cc and a new cub Elegant 110 which caught everyone's attention. Besides, we had held a presentation of new Latin America models for our own distributors.

In this show, we want not only to promote our brand but show our ambitions and confidence to enlarge our Colombia market in the future.
